Kibble

Meet all of your nutritional needs with one small pellet!

Kibble is a palatable meal that is usually available 24/7. It has been keeping me alive for a decade!

Making this dish takes 1-30 minutes depending on the attentiveness of your human.

Here's what you need:

  • Kibble, 150g
  • Bowl, any size
  • Full bladder (optional)

Here's what you do:

  1. Check to see if your human is awake.
  2. If they are still sleeping, meow loudly in close proximity to their face.
  3. If they do not acknowledge you, walk on top of them (ideally on the head or stomach).
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until they wake up.
    If you have come prepared with a full bladder, you may empty it on your human's pillow now to expedite this process.
  5. Lead them to the kitchen. You may need to stop, stare, and meow at them throughout this step as they are often easily distracted.
  6. Increase meow volume as you watch them scoop the kibble into your bowl.
  7. Enjoy!
    *Note* If you need a little extra entertainment, double your consumption speed. This will allow you to immediately purge the
    undigested kibble which you can then watch your human clean up.
